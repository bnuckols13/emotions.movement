🗣️ The Body Speaks in Movement

Your body is constantly communicating through sensation, tension, and energy. Emotions aren't just thoughts - they're full-body experiences.

"Movement can help us translate what feelings are trying to tell us. ED behaviors often silence the body's voice - today we practice listening."

🧭 Emotional GPS System

😤

Anger/Frustration

Tension, heat, wanting to push away

Try: Pushing motions, stamping feet
😢

Sadness/Grief

Heavy, droopy, like melting or sinking

Try: Slow, downward movements, folding inward
😰

Anxiety/Fear

Fast, jittery, scattered, wanting to escape

Try: Quick movements, protective gestures
😊

Joy/Excitement

Light, bouncy, expansive, reaching outward

Try: Jumping, reaching up, opening arms
😶

Numbness/Shutdown

Disconnected, heavy, hard to move

Try: Very slow movements, exploring stillness

🧘‍♀️ Your Body's Wisdom

  • Each emotion has information for you - even the uncomfortable ones
  • Movement helps emotions flow through instead of getting stuck
  • There's no 'wrong' way to feel or move - only your authentic expression
  • Today we practice being curious instead of judgmental about what we feel
